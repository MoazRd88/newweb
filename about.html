About Prof. Dr. Hasan Engin Sener

My research areas are focused on Human Resource Management, Strategic Management, Public Policy Analysis, and Public Management.

Throughout my career, I have carried out numerous projects for various institutions, including the United Nations Development Programme (UNDP), the Ministry of Interior, the Ministry of Trade, and the Ministry of Development. In addition to my academic work, I also provide training and seminars for public personnel, such as those from the Ministry of Interior, Ministry of National Education, and Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

I hold a certification as an Internal Auditor for ISO 9001 and am also a certified External Evaluator for the Turkish Higher Education Quality Council. Currently, I am teaching courses on Strategic Management and Human Resource Management at Ankara Yıldırım Beyazıt University, as well as Management Techniques at Ahmet Yesevi University. I am also a member of the Alumni of the Faculty of Political Sciences (Mülkiyeliler Birliği), METU Alumni Organization (ODTÜ Mezunlar Derneği), and the Personnel Management Association of Turkey (PERYÖN).

Thank you for visiting my website and please feel free to contact me for any inquiries or further information.
